The Good Steak

A delicious steak that is cooked in many ways and uses the good seasoning.

    Ingredients

  • whatever cut of steak you like, my prefrence is sirloin.
  • the good seasoning

    Directions

  1. Dry rub your steak with some of the good seasoning
  2. Now cook your steak however you wish, grill it, cook it on the stove, or even stick it in the oven, you are in control here.

Sorry about the lackluster directions and description but, I don't think it can be better, the recipies I made are meant to be done as people wish.
