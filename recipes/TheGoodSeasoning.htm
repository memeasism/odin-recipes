The Good Seasoning

This is a guide on how to make a very basic season that I love so much I put it on any food I make, whether a little bit or a full dry rub. This seasoning is what you want it to be, I literally guess amounts but, when its done it tastes different to everyone but, it's always good. This is a peppery seasoning with many different types of peppers all mixed together for peppery goodness.

    Ingredients

  • paprika
  • cayenne
  • ground pepper
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • salt

    Directions

  1. I would consider this a libre recipe so just add until you think it tastes amazing.
  2. I would start by doing about 0.5 tbsp. of salt and 4 tbsp. of garlic and onion powder.
  3. Now you want to start adding the rest, I suggest 2 tbsp. of ground pepper, and 8 of cayenne or 8 of paprika, because their flavor is so similar I suggest 8 tbsp. of one or 4 tbsp. of each.

I will possibly update this with what the actual amounts I suggest is, this is just guessing based of the ratio of how it usually goes when I make it.
