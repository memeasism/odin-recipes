White Chicken Chilli Soup

White Chicken Chilli is a creamy soup with plenty of beans, pepper(seasoning), and chicken. White Chicken is a mildly spicy dish so if you don't like spicy you should avoid it. I would say White Chicken Chilli is perfect for if you are in the mood for soup but also spicy food as, I dont know many spicy soups.

    Ingredients

  • 24 oz. shredded chicken
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 can of chicken broth
  • white kidney beans
  • 1 can of diced green chiles
  • 1 tsp. of oregano
  • 1 tsp. of cumin
  • 1 tsp. of cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • mozzarella cheese

    Directions

  1. If you couldnt find shredded chicken, prepare by shredding any chicken that you will be using.
  2. Pour all the indredients but the cheese, broth, and cream.
  3. Pour in the broth
  4. Cook for a while and then begin to pour in the cream, be sure to stir often to prevent food from settling.
  5. When the broth and cream seem to have had mixed well, turn off the stove and top it off with some cheese
  6. ENJOY
